Born in the embrace of the Doon Valley, ZIBA is more than a cafe. It is a chronicle of warmth, skill, and the art of slow living.

A Journey of Two Hearts

In October 2020, amidst the quiet majesty of the Doon Valley, Nirdesh and Ruby envisioned a space that breathed. They sought to build something that wasn't just a destination, but an anchor for the community.

ZIBA was founded on the belief that beauty lies in the details—the grain of the wood, the depth of the roast, and the genuine smile of the person serving you.

"We wanted to create a hearth for the valley—a place where time slows down and every cup tells a story of the soil it came from."
The Soul of ZIBA
